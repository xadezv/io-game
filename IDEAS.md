# Gameplay Ideas

## 1) Blizzard Fronts
- **Description:** Dynamic blizzard zones periodically sweep through snow biomes, reducing visibility and draining temperature faster unless players stay near campfires or wear winter hats.
- **Complexity:** medium

## 2) Tribal Totems
- **Description:** Placeable team totems that grant nearby allies small buffs (faster crafting, minor regen), but can be destroyed by enemies to deny control.
- **Complexity:** high

## 3) Resource Caravan Events
- **Description:** Random neutral caravans move across the map carrying loot. Players can escort them for rewards or ambush them for risky PvP.
- **Complexity:** high

## 4) Fishing at Water Edge
- **Description:** Add fishing spots near ocean borders for food and rare crafting drops, creating safer early-game progression paths.
- **Complexity:** low

## 5) Night Predator Surge
- **Description:** During night cycle, predator spawn chance increases and elite variants appear briefly, rewarding extra XP/resources for survival.
- **Complexity:** medium

## 6) Portable Bedroll
- **Description:** Craftable bedroll allows one-time faster respawn with reduced penalties, adding tactical preparation before raids.
- **Complexity:** low
